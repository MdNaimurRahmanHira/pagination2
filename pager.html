With R, Python, Jupyter (or almost any development language) and Monitor+, almost any analytics can be applied to your market data and ad-hoc analytic reports created quickly and easily. Averages, correlations, seasonality, curves, ARIMA, machine learning, forecasting and thousands more - all are readily available. There are no limits to the number of reports you can create - you can even integrate your own data.

Give your traders and analysts a big commercial advantage by helping them to better analyse the markets with your own custom analytic reports.
